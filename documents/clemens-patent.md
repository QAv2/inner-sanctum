## Engineering Specifications

### Medium
- Mercury doped with thorium dioxide
- Doping ratio: 3 parts thorium to 100,000 parts mercury (by weight)

### Atmosphere
- 100% argon
- Evacuated to 6-10 torr

### Electrical Parameters
- Minimum: 150 kilovolts per plasma chamber
- Preferred: in excess of 1 million volts
- Pulse duration: no greater than 100 microseconds

### Configurations

**First Embodiment (mechanical):**
- Two stacked counter-rotating plasma cylinders
- Mechanically driven by motors
- Spindle electrodes in bearings
- Brushes for electrode energization
- Surrounding induction coil
- Boron carbide neutron shield

**Preferred Embodiment (no moving parts):**
- Two concentric plasma chambers
- Rotation achieved by sequentially energizing fixed biased electrodes
- Fixed concentric magnets at top and bottom
- Induction coil between rotating plasmas
- More stable, no mechanical wear

### Shielding
- Boron carbide neutron shield (cylindrical ceramic)

### Energy Extraction
- Induction coil surrounding rotating plasmas (Faraday's law)

## Claims
1. Extracts useful energy from the zero-point field
2. Modifies or nullifies the forces of gravity and inertia, or the properties of mass
3. Creates a "gravity, inertia and mass canceled envelope"
4. Gravity described as "an induced effect resulting from the interaction of ZPE with subatomic charge"
5. Coherence with zero-point field allows interference with gravitational interaction
