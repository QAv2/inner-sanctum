### DIRD #05 — Advanced Space Propulsion Based on Vacuum (Spacetime Metric) Engineering
**Author**: Hal Puthoff (EarthTech) | **ArXiv**: https://arxiv.org/abs/1204.2184

- Gravitational effects = changes in vacuum permittivity (ε₀) and permeability (μ₀)
- Variable K (dielectric constant of vacuum) is the key parameter
- Speed of light varies as c/K in presence of mass/energy
- Reproduces all three classical GR tests to observational accuracy
- **Reframes Alcubierre warp drive as vacuum EM engineering problem**, not exotic matter problem
- ZPF (zero-point field) is the fundamental driving principle

**RS translation**: Puthoff's variable K = Larson's scalar motion magnitude. Manipulating K = manipulating the space-time ratio. The PV model IS the Reciprocal System expressed in conventional physics notation without knowing it.
