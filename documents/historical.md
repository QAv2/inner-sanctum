### The "Fly Trap" / Henge at Ludwikowice
- Concrete dodecagonal frame atop 12 pillars, ~30m diameter, ~10m high
- Near Wenceslas Mine, Ludwikowice Klodzkie (fmr Ludwigsdorf), Lower Silesia, Poland
- Built 1943-44. Witkowski says it was the test rig.
- Mainstream assessment: probably a cooling tower base
- Now maintained by Molke Museum, open to visitors

### Hans Kammler — The Missing General
- SS-Obergruppenführer, commanded all V-weapons programs by 1945
- Answerable only to Himmler
- Vanished May 1945 — three contradictory death accounts, body never found
- Name appears only 26 times in 35,000 pages of Nuremberg transcripts
- **Wilson Center (2019)**: CIC investigator Packe concluded Kammler "was in American custody"
- **Donald Richardson** (CIC/OSS, Alsos Mission): told sons he "brought Kammler to the US," supervised him until 1947
- **Dean Reuter et al., *The Hidden Nazi***: US documents prove Kammler was in American custody months after his "suicide"
- Wilson Center paper: https://www.wilsoncenter.org/publication/hans-kammler-hitlers-last-hope-american-hands

### Evidentiary Status
- **No primary documents exist** — no blueprints, photographs, test records in any captured German archive
- Entire account traces to single chain (Witkowski → unnamed contact → Sporrenberg transcripts)
- Sporrenberg trial transcripts not independently verified or publicly released
- However: Kammler capture now documented by credible institution (Wilson Center)
- The ABSENCE of documentation is itself suspicious given thorough Allied cataloging of other Wunderwaffen

## Vimana Texts (Vaimanika Shastra)

### Provenance
- Revealed 1952 by G.R. Josyer
- Dictated by Pandit Subbaraya Shastry (1866-1940) between 1918-1923
- Claimed to be psychically channeled from sage Bharadvaja
- **Modern composition** (early 20th century) regardless of claimed ancient source
- 3,000 shlokas in 8 chapters

### Mercury Vortex Engine
- Chamber filled with liquid mercury
- Electromagnetic coils generate magnetic field in chamber
- EM field induces vortex in mercury
- Electric current through mercury generates secondary EM field
- Interaction produces propulsive force
- Uses gyroscopes, electricity, and mercury together

### Academic Assessment
- 1974 study (Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore): aircraft described are "aeronautically unfeasible," principles "largely perfunctory and incorrect"
- However: the mercury + EM + vortex configuration matches independently derived designs

### Significance for This Project
Not credible as historical document. Highly significant as convergent design — someone in early 20th century India described the same device architecture that appears in WWII Germany, 1990s Finland, and 2023 US patents.

## Otis T. Carr (OTC-X1)

### Background
- Otis T. Carr (1904-1982), claimed Tesla protege
- Developed OTC-X1 anti-gravity spacecraft

### Technical Claims
- Spinning containers of liquid mercury
- Counter-rotating plates + high voltage
- "Utron Electric Accumulator" — geometric energy device
- Accumulator + 6 capacitor plates rotate one direction
- External platform + 12 electromagnets rotate opposite direction
- Central cabin remains stationary (coupling bearings)

### April 19, 1959 Demonstration
- Crowds gathered at Oklahoma City amusement park
- Scheduled: unmanned 6-foot OTC-X1 to reach 500 feet
- Carr hospitalized with "mysterious illness" — mentioned a "mercury leak"
- Demonstration never occurred
- Carr later imprisoned for selling stock illegally

### Significance
Another convergent data point: counter-rotating elements, mercury, high voltage, claimed gravitational effects. Carr's mercury leak hospitalization parallels Die Glocke's lethal radiation effects — consistent with hazardous operation of the same type of device.
