# Salvatore Pais Navy Patents

## Overview
Five patents filed by the US Navy (2015-2018) through inventor Salvatore Cezar Pais. All three granted patents have expired due to non-payment of maintenance fees. Two applications were abandoned.

## Patent List

### 1. US10135366B2 — Electromagnetic Field Generator
- **Filed**: July 24, 2015 | **Granted**: Nov 20, 2018 | **Expired**: Nov 20, 2022
- Shell with embedded PZT (lead zirconate titanate) ceramic
- Pelletron electrostatic generators charging to 30 megavolts
- Nuclear pebble bed reactor (1-10 MW)
- Rotation at ~30,000 RPM + vibration at 10⁹-10¹⁸ Hz
- Claims EM field intensities of 10²⁴ W/m²

### 2. US10144532B2 — Craft Using an Inertial Mass Reduction Device (THE flagship patent)
- **Filed**: April 28, 2016 | **Granted**: Dec 4, 2018 | **Expired**: Jan 9, 2023
- Hybrid Aerospace/Undersea Craft (HAUC) — conical with elliptical cross-section
- Inner/outer resonant cavity walls with microwave emitters (300 MHz–300 GHz)
- Xenon-filled resonant cavity
- Surface charge density: 50,000 C/m²
- Rotation: 30,000 RPM; vibration coupling to 10¹⁸ Hz
- EM intensity: 10²⁴ to 10²⁸ W/m²
- Creates "local polarized vacuum" → inertial mass reduction
- "Vacuum/plasma bubble/sheath" enclosing craft
- CTO James Sheehy personally intervened with USPTO to get it granted
- Sheehy warned China was "investing significantly in this area"

### 3. US10322827B2 — High Frequency Gravitational Wave Generator
- **Filed**: Feb 14, 2017 | **Granted**: Jun 18, 2019 | **Expired**: Jun 18, 2023
- Two acoustic resonant gas-filled cavities that **counter-spin** relative to each other
- Based on Gertsenshtein Effect (photon-graviton mixing)
- Claims 10³³ W/m² EM energy flux → vacuum polarization

### 4. US20190058105A1 — Piezoelectricity-Induced Room Temperature Superconductor
- **Filed**: Aug 16, 2017 | **Abandoned**: Nov 12, 2019
- Wire with insulator core + PZT coating at London penetration depth thickness
- Claims room-temp superconductivity via accelerated vibration
- No experimental data included

### 5. US20190295733A1 — Plasma Compression Fusion Device
- **Filed**: Mar 22, 2018 | **Abandoned**: Jul 14, 2021
- Compact fusion reactor (0.3-2m diameter)
- Counter-spinning dynamic fusors (tungsten alloy)
- Rotation up to 100,000+ RPM
- Claims energy gain ~10¹⁸
- Navy PowerPoint (FOIA): could lead to "Spacetime Modification Weapon"

## Key Analysis Points

**No mercury** — Pais uses xenon gas, PZT ceramic, no mercury anywhere.
**No bell shape** — Conical/lenticular, not bell.
**Structural parallel to Die Glocke**: Both use counter-rotating elements, high-energy EM fields, claimed spacetime effects. Different materials, same principle.

**The CTO intervention is the most significant data point.** A senior Navy official personally vouched for operability and warned about Chinese competition. Either the Navy genuinely believes this physics has merit, or it's information warfare. The $508K in actual testing suggests genuine interest.

**All patents expired/abandoned** — the Navy stopped paying fees. This could mean: (a) the technology doesn't work, (b) it was moved to a classified program and the public patents are no longer needed, or (c) the technology was deemed not worth protecting at this time.
